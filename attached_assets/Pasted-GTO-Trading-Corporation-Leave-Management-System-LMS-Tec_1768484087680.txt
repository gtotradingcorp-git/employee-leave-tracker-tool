GTO Trading Corporation
Leave Management System (LMS)

Technical Documentation

1. Overview

The GTO Trading Corporation Leave Management System (LMS) is a centralized, web-based application designed to streamline employee leave filing, approval, monitoring, and reporting across all departments. The system primarily supports operational teams (Logistics, Frontline, Warehouse) while remaining fully accessible to all departments and management levels.

The LMS ensures accuracy, transparency, and efficiency in leave management for approximately 200 employees, from rank-and-file to executive-level positions.

2. Objectives

Automate employee leave filing and approval workflows

Maintain a centralized and auditable employee leave database

Enable real-time reporting for HR, IT & Digital Transformation, and Top Management

Enforce corporate email registration security

Improve operational efficiency and managerial oversight

7. Leave Filing Process
7.1 Leave Application Form

Employees must complete the following:

Type of Leave (dropdown list)

Department (dropdown list)

Start Date (calendar picker)

End Date (calendar picker)

Total Number of Leave Days (auto-calculated)

Reason for Leave (text field)

File Upload (optional / required for specific leave types)

Date & Time of Filing (auto-generated by system)

7.2 Calendar Integration

Interactive calendar for selecting leave dates

Prevents invalid date selections

Highlights overlapping or previously approved leaves

8. Approval Workflow

Employee submits leave request

Manager reviews and either:

Approves

Rejects (with remarks)

Approved leaves are:

Included in automated reports

Notifications are sent to:
